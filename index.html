Welcome to the Rizal Park Tourism Website!

The Rizal Park, aka the Luneta Park, is an urban park in Ermita, Manila. The site is incredibly huge, as it covers an area of 58 hectares. Originally called Bagumbayan, the location was notorious for the public executions of Filipinos who opposed Spanish rule. Notable Filipinos are our national hero, Jose Rizal, hence the namesake of the place and GOMBURZA.
