In October 1989, husband and wife team Boni and Rose Pimentel launched Ilustrado, the first find dining restaurant in the Walled City of Intramuros. These “original foodies,” and partners in both life and business, were inspired by the passion for fine dining to open a restaurant where discerning diners could sample both Filipino-Spanish and modern global cuisine of the highest caliber. https://ilustradorestaurant.ph/

Address: 744 General Luna St., Intramuros, Manila
Hours: 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM



Café Ilang-Ilang is the epicenter of Manila’s power dining, it has been a favorite spot for generations. Café Ilang-Ilang is a renaissance of chic casual dining which brings together a vast array of cuisines from across all continents. Designed artfully to satisfy the distinguishing palate, the spacious restaurant features an extensive selection of buffet delights with a fresh spin on its various open kitchens and a delectable desserts space that highlights its tradition of unique and excellent dining. Its eight live cooking stations offer Asian, Mediterranean, European, and Western dishes. Café Ilang-Ilang also has a stunning open Pan-Asian kitchen, Churrasco station, decadent dessert station, and an Italian kitchen where awesome pizza and pastas are made for a truly unique gastronomic adventure. https://www.manila-hotel.com.ph/dining/cafe-ilang-ilang/

Address: Manila Hotel, 1 Rizal Park, Ermita, Manila, 0913 Metro Manila
Hours: 6:00 AM – 9:30 PM
